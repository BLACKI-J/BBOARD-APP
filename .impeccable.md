# BBOARD Design Context

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: Directors (Management, oversight, high-level organization).
- **Secondary**: Animateurs (Field use, planning consultation, exit sheet management).
- **Context**: Mobile/Tablet on the field (need speed and clarity) and Desktop in the office (data-heavy recap).

## Use Cases
- **Van Planning**: Managing transportation logistics efficiently.
- **Planning Recap**: Quick overview of the day/week activities.
- **Exit Sheets (Fiches de Sortie)**: Critical safety and compliance documentation.
- **Directory**: Accessing contact and medical info in seconds.

## Brand Personality & Tone
- **Personality**: "Utilitarian Playfulness"
- **Tone**: Professional and ultra-fast (efficiency first), but vibrant and colorful to reflect the "Colo" spirit.
- **Constraints**: Avoid high-saturation "eye-straining" palettes. Use balanced, curated colors that feel alive but stable.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Style**: Liquid Glass / Modern Neumorphism
- **Emphasis**: Clear hierarchy, staggered animations for "delight", and high-performance interactions.
